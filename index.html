About Me

I am a machine vision engineer specializing in developing turnkey machine vision applications for a variety of customers mainly in the life sciences industries. I take pride in being involved with projects from design to delivery and love the challenges presented by this field.

I studied Mechanical Engineering at the University of Guelph where I graduated with distinction. I discovered machine vision through the University's co-op program where I interned with ATS Automation. I have also obtained a Lean Six Sigma Green Belt as part of an effort to implement lean methodologies in my work.

In my spare time I enjoy cooking, reading and airsoft.
